# Recommended Reading (Intermediate - Senior)

Overview
---
Curated reading for candidates preparing for senior backend roles.

Books & Resources
---
- "Domain-Driven Design" by Eric Evans — Core DDD concepts and strategic design.
- "Designing Data-Intensive Applications" by Martin Kleppmann — Distributed systems fundamentals.
- Marten & Wolverine documentation — Practical patterns for .NET event stores and messaging.
- PostgreSQL: Up and Running / High Performance PostgreSQL guides — Practical DBA skills for production.

Focus Areas
---
- Aggregates and invariants (DDDs tactical patterns).
- Event sourcing and projections.
- Scaling and partitioning strategies for relational databases.
- Observability and incident response patterns.
