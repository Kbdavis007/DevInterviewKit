# Behavioral Questions: Leadership & Architecture (Senior)

Overview
---
A set of behavioral prompts designed to uncover leadership, mentoring, and decision-making skills.

Prompts
---
1. Describe a time you made an unpopular technical decision. How did you communicate it and what was the outcome?
2. Explain how you onboard a new engineer to a complex codebase and ensure they become productive quickly.
3. A critical production incident demands a fast fix that will reduce long-term reliability. How do you proceed?

Evaluation Criteria
---
- Clarity of communication and trade-off reasoning.
- Evidence of mentoring and knowledge transfer.
- Ability to prioritize long-term quality over short-term pressure where appropriate.

Follow-up Questions
---
- How do you decide when to stop a refactor and ship?
- How do you handle persistent disagreements on an architecture team?
